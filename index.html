A Letter From My Heart 💌

Happy Girlfriend's Day ❤️

My Dearest EBUNMI,

As I sit here trying to put my heart into words, I realize something almost impossible: no matter how beautifully I write, it will never fully capture how much you mean to me. Still, I want to try, because you deserve to know just how deeply you are loved

Before you came into my life, I thought I understood what love was. I thought it was just a feeling—something people talked about in songs and stories. Then I met you, and I discovered that love isn't just something you feel; it's something you become.

One of the many things I admire most about you is your kindness. It's not the kind of kindness people put on for the world to see. It's genuine. It's in the way you care about others, the way you listen without judgment, the way you give without expecting anything in return, and the way you make people feel seen and valued. Your heart has taught me that true strength isn't found in being the loudest person in the room but in having the gentlest soul

Loving you has changed me in ways I never expected. You have inspired me to become more patient, more understanding, more intentional, and more compassionate. Because of you, I think more carefully about my words, I love more deeply, and I dream bigger. You make me want to become the kind of man who is worthy of the incredible woman standing beside him

You have shown me that love is found in the little things—the conversations that last for hours, the laughter we share over nothing, the comfort of simply knowing you're there, and the quiet moments that become unforgettable because they're spent with you.

Thank you for believing in me even when I struggled to believe in myself. Thank you for encouraging me to keep growing, for celebrating my victories, comforting me through my disappointments, and reminding me that I never have to face life alone. Your love has been one of the greatest blessings I have ever received

If there is one promise I hope you always remember, it is this: I will never stop choosing you. I will choose you on the easy days and on the difficult ones. I will choose you when we are laughing together and when life tests us. I will choose you every single day because loving you has become one of the greatest privileges of my life

Thank you for being exactly who you are. Never stop being kind. Never stop shining your light. The world is brighter because you're in it, and my life is infinitely better because I get to love you.

Happy Girlfriend's Day, my beautiful Queen

Today, tomorrow, and every day after, my heart belongs to you

Forever Yours, ADEGOKE ❤️
